Discover Kenilworth’s History

Explore the Kenilworth People & Places Walking Trail and uncover the historic landmarks, stories, and locations that have shaped the town over the centuries.

Trail Information

Distance

Approximately 3 miles (4.8 km)

Duration

1.5 – 2 hours walking time

Difficulty

Easy to moderate

Starting Point

Kenilworth Town Centre

About the Trail

The Kenilworth People & Places Walking Trail highlights key locations across the town that reflect its historical development and cultural heritage. The route takes visitors through important landmarks and areas associated with notable people and events in Kenilworth’s past.

This digital version of the trail leaflet provides an accessible way for visitors and residents to explore the town while learning about its history and character.

Accessibility Information

The walking trail is approximately 3 miles in length and generally follows pavements and public pathways through Kenilworth town centre.

Some sections may include uneven surfaces, narrow pavements, or gentle slopes. Visitors are advised to wear suitable footwear and allow additional time if travelling with children or mobility aids.
